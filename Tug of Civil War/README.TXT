PROJECT TITLE: Tug of Civil War
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Student Research and Scholarship Day 2016
VERSION or DATE: 4-6-2016
HOW TO START THIS PROJECT: Push Act on the promt to start the game. Press ENTER to view how to play the game. Press ENTER again to start the game.
AUTHORS: Benjamin Lipscomb, Michelle Fernandez, Dr. Brian Canada
USER INSTRUCTIONS: Move the Union soldier with the arrow keys, press spacebar to fire.
Kill the enemy to make the rope move towards you. If the rope comes completely to your side you
get a gatling gun. Use the W, A, S, D keys to move it. Try and kill the enemy commander to win the game.
If your commander dies, you lose the game. If you die, you lose the game as well.
